Ahoy, sailors! Between regattas, BNAC, classes, weddings, dock building, sauna renovation, and grounds clean-up it’s been one of the busiest summers at the ASC I can remember. So busy, in fact, that I’ve been too distracted to get the normal summer ASC updates out.

But I’ve vowed to get started again, and I’m kicking off the effort with some recent regatta results:

- **Ice Breaker Results**: Because of our ongoing dock-building work, the Ice Breaker was constrained to a single day. And it was gloriously sunny that day … and also windless. But we unanimously voted to award Jim and Laura Aumann 1st place based on the fact that they were the only team at the regatta with matching sparkles.

- **Firecracker / BNAC Warmup Results**: For the Bucc Fleet, 1st place went to Steve Ryan & Will Merriman, 2nd place to Cathy & Geoff Wright, and 3rd to Gabe Black and Adrianna Ramirez. For the Open Fleet, Bart Hawkins took 1st and Kaiden & Amelia Foster took 2nd. TL nailed the top of the podium as the one and only boat in the keelboat fleet. (Some other big boat owners need to come out and give him some competition next time.) Full results are available [here](/uploads/2025/08/firecracker-2025-results.pdf), and thanks to Tom Harrison, Camille Oliver, and Paul Willing for the race committee work.

- **Buccaneer 18 North American Championship Results**: In A Fleet, John Weiss & Jay Foght took 1st, Ed Mantano & Shannon Devine took 2nd, and Evan & Elizabeth Scott took 3rd. In B Fleet, Trevor & Rachel Bach took 1st, Gabe Black & Adrianna Ramirez took 2nd, and Geoff & Cathy Wright took 3rd. Hometown heroes Steve Ryan and Will Merriman took 6th in A Fleet, and Brie Busey and Oliver Wright took 8th in A Fleet. Full results are available on the [Buccaneer 18 class website](https://www.buccaneer18.org/content.aspx?page_id=22&club_id=405653&module_id=690839). We had a big crew of ASC folks helping with race committee duties, including Tom Harrison, Bob Chivvis, "The Jims" (Jim Aumann and Jim Johnson), Paul Willing, and Raia Stam. Mike Ferring led our visiting race officials and set an amazing standard with both his officiating and on-water announcing.

- **Pirate Race Results**: The Christensen clan organized a happy and rowdy Pirate Race, and the mighty Ensign (crewed by Steve Ryan, Jake Black, Ray Delay, and David "New Guy" Kulikovskiy) took top honors. They were aided by the fact that winds were light and the rules didn’t explicitly forbid the use of electric motors. Highlights of the day included the catered Hawaiian dinner, the impressive number of young pirates in attendance, and a double birthday, with Charles McAdams turning 21 and Jack Torres turning 11. As per recent tradition, participating pirates were prepared to draw straws to determine the hosts of the next Pirate Race, but the Torres clan decided to volunteer for the happy job. We can't wait to see what they have in store for next year!
