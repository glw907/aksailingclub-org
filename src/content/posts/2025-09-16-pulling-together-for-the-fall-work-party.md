This season has been a banner year for volunteer effort at the Alaska Sailing Club. Together, members have:

- Hosted BNAC 2025, the first-ever international sailing event in Alaska

- Hosted three sailing classes with an all-volunteer instructor crew

- Built three brand-new docks and framed three more

- Worked on countless upgrades to the ASC fleet

- Repainted the clubhouse floor and completed a major sauna refresh

- Poured new footings for our awnings

- Added a new event grill and custom taps

- Hosted a wedding for club members Hannah and Duncan Wright

- Hauled away *3.7 tons* of old junk from the grounds

- Built a new kayak rack and removed the old Lido rack

- Removed the old Lido rack to make more space for boat parking

It’s an incredible list of accomplishments and shows the power and commitment of the ASC community. (I had to double-check this list after writing it. Did we *really* do all that?) But even after all this volunteer love, we’re not quite done. We just need one more push at the fall work party to get everything buttoned up for winter.

### Here’s the plan:

The work party starts at 10:00 AM on Sunday the 21st. (The day after the Northern Lights Regetta, so you can make a weekend of it.) When you arrive, find Cathy Wright. She’ll have the task list and will match you with a “project captain” to get you started. We’re usually finished by 4:00 PM, sometimes earlier, if we get a strong turnout.

You *don’t* need to stay all day. Even an hour or two of help makes a real difference.

**Core tasks for the day**:

- Folding in the docks and finishing dock framing

- Installing landscape edging along the shoreline

- Winterizing boats and engines

- Pulling the Ensign and pontoon boat

- Cleaning and organizing the clubhouse and sauna

- Grounds prep by the old Lido rack

- Bucking and stacking wood

- Cleaning out flower beds

- Planting two new trees

- …plus a handful of smaller projects

### What to bring:

- All-weather work clothes

- Work gloves

- Boots or mud-friendly shoes

If you’re a “tool person,” please bring your gear. (You guys know who you are!)

### Wednesday planning meeting:

The Site Committee will meet virtually at 6:30 PM in the `#meetings` channel on the ASC Discord server to finalize the task list. We’ll use Discord’s built-in video feature. If you’re new to this, see [How to Use Discord Video Calls](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/360041721052-Video-Calls). You can also review our [ASC Discord Server info page](/members/discord-server/) for details on joining the server.

No worries if you can’t make the meeting. We’ll also publish an editable Google Sheets task list. If you have input, just post it in the `#site` channel on Discord.

It’s been a big year for volunteering, and many members have already stepped up for impromptu work days. Hopefully we can rally one more time. (And don’t miss the Northern Lights Regatta on Saturday, so you can make it a full weekend of ASC friends and fun. We’ll have SIs for the Northern Lights up tomorrow.)
