The Alaska Sailing Club is pleased to announce the **Spring Racing Series 2026**. Registration is now open for all club members and qualified visiting sailors.

**Series Schedule:**
- May 24: Icebreaker Regatta (series opener)
- June 7: Spring Challenge Race
- June 21: Summer Solstice Classic

**Registration Details:**
- Entry fee: $50 for ASC members, $75 for non-members
- Registration deadline: May 17, 2026
- Online registration available through MembershipWorks
- Includes post-race social and awards ceremony

Questions? Contact the Race Committee at racing@aksailingclub.org or visit the clubhouse during open hours.
