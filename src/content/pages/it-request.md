---
title: "IT & Infrastructure Requests"
---

Need help with your club email, website login, Discord access, or something not working right on the site? Here's how to reach us.

For boat, dock, or clubhouse issues, use [Issues & Support](/issues-and-support/) instead.

:::callout[Requesting IT Help]{tone="note"}
Post in `#technology` on Discord, or email `board@aksailingclub.org`, describing the account,
access, or website problem.
:::
