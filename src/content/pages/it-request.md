---
title: "IT & Infrastructure Requests"
---

Need help with your club email, website login, Discord access, or something not working right on the site? Submit a request below and we'll get back to you.

For boat, dock, or clubhouse issues, use [Issues & Support](/issues-and-support/) instead. You can also reach us in `#technology` on [Discord](/discord-server/).

:::callout{tone="note"}
[Requesting IT help]
The request form isn't wired up here yet. In the meantime, post in `#technology` on Discord, or
email `board@aksailingclub.org` describing the account, access, or website problem.
:::
