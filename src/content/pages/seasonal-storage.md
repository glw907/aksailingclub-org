The ASC offers seasonal storage for members who want to keep a boat or RV at the club. These arrangements, moorings, long-term RV parking, trailered boat parking, and rack storage, are available for active, participating members in good standing.

Most options have waitlists, and demand is high. If you're interested in a seasonal spot, the best path is to get involved in club activities first: race, volunteer, attend work parties. Members who actively contribute to the club get priority consideration when spots open up.

## Membership Requirement

All seasonal storage is reserved for **Active Participating Members**, as defined in the [Member Expectations](/member-expectations/) policy. This status has two components.

**Member in Good Standing.** You're current on dues and have contributed to at least one work party during the year (or a board-approved substitute project).

**Active Participating Member.** You've also done at least one of the following within the past year:

- Participated in at least two ASC events as a volunteer, racer, or race committee
- Worked in a volunteer capacity to forward the club's non-profit mission

**Important:** The ASC is not a secure facility. All personal property is kept at the owner's risk. The club may need to move stored property as necessary for club activities.

## Storage Options

The club offers four types of seasonal storage, ranging from the most in-demand (moorings) to generally available without a long wait (rack storage). Each option has its own guidelines: click through for full details on requirements, fees, and how to get on a waitlist.
