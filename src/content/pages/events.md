title: "Events"
---

The ASC season runs from Memorial Day through Labor Day, with regattas, Wet Wednesdays, classes, potlucks, and work parties throughout the summer. Most events are family-friendly weekends with camping, campfires, and plenty of time on the water.

:::callout{tone="note"}

The full events calendar reads from the club's own event data and isn't wired up on this page yet
(a separate build step). Check `#racing` and `#general` on [Discord](/discord-server/) for the
latest event announcements in the meantime.
:::
