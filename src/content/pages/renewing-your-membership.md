title: "Renewing Your Membership"
---

Renewing is quick: log in to your account, confirm your membership type, and pay. If you're past due, your benefits are on hold but your account stays open. Seasonal storage (moorings, RV, boat parking, rack) renews separately. Contact the Harbormaster about moorings, or the Site Committee Chair for everything else, through our [contact form](/contact/).

:::callout[Renew Your Membership]{tone="note"}
[Log in to your account](/my-account/) and complete payment. No approval needed.
:::

## Questions

**Changing membership type** (Individual to Family, or similar): pick the new type when you renew; the price updates before you pay.

**Payment or billing issues:** use the [Issues & Support](/issues-and-support/?category=billing#report-an-issue) form.

**Trouble signing in?** There's no password; your sign-in link arrives by email. If it doesn't, [contact us](/contact/).
